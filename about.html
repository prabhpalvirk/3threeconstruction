Our Story

Built on Trust. Driven by Excellence.

Founded in 2023, 3three Construction and Landscape was established with a singular vision: to raise the standard of residential construction and outdoor spaces in Brisbane.

Based in Calamvale, Queensland, we proudly service our local community and surrounding suburbs. We understand that undertaking a building or landscaping project is a major investment. That is why we treat every property with the utmost respect.

We do not just see our customers as clients. We see them as partners. Our goal is to work collaboratively with you, ensuring that the final outcome exceeds your expectations in both durability and design.

Our Core Values

Precision Craftsmanship

We do not cut corners. Whether we are pouring concrete or laying turf, attention to detail is guaranteed.

Transparent Communication

As your building partners, we keep you informed at every stage. No hidden fees, no unexpected delays.

Enduring Quality

We source premium materials and utilize industry-leading techniques to ensure our work stands the test of time.
